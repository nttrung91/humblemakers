April 15th 2014

Today was exciting since we were going to cross the bridge from designing to programming! We went to EVOKE lab to discuss problems our app needs to solve and solutions the app provides.

Brainstorming

We started our design progress by storyboarding. We wrote down challenges for austic kids and parents individually and post them on the whiteboard

Solution

After we finished brainstorming, we clarified our thoughts into categories of expression, capture memories, assurance and engaging. Since autistic kids can not express themselves well, they can not get connected to others. Therefore, we want to develop an application that speaks out heir inner voice!

Collaboration

After we discussed about how to implement our application, we split the work according to our specialities.
