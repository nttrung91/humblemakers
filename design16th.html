Storyboarding, personas and UI model

April 16th-20th 2014

We created personas of our target users, drew storyboard, finalized our UI and divided our whole coding to modules.

Personas

We created two personas. One is an 8 years old girl Lynda and the other is a 44 years old mother Kathy, whose autistic kid is Rosa. Lynda and Kathy undergo challenges for kids and parents accordingly. For example, Lynda She likes to eat food and wear clothes from a specific color. Mother Kathy has to repeat herself many times so Rosa can understand

Storyboarding

For storyboarding, we drew a failed case where the teacher can not calm down Lynda who cried suddenly in class. We also drew a successful case in which teacher passes our application to Lynda. She gets attracted by the beautiful icons and stops crying. Since she can express herself freely with our application, she gets a sense of control and enjoys playing it.

Finalized UI

At first, our UI was the first on the left. However, we realized that although the left design will look nice on the phone, it will look funny on the computer. We stucked in this obstable for a while. Then, we defined our target users, who will use tablets more. After that, Cubi came up with the design on the right, which works perfect on devices with bigger width than height! We can see it is very instuitive from the similation of eyeball movement.
